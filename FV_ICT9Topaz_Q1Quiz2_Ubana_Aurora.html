Cars

In a world populated by anthropomorphic vehicles, the Dinoco 400 race marks the climax of the Piston Cup season.
Lightning McQueen. Desperate to win and gain entry into the prestigious Dinoco team, Lightning struggles with teamwork
due to his self-centered attitude. After the race, Lightning rushes to reach California inside his transport truck Mack.
A mishap leaves Lightning stranded alone in the rundown desert town of Radiator Springs.
Here, he finds the true meaning of friendship and family.


The Cast

  • Lightning McQueen (Owen Wilson)

    • Lightning McQueen is the protagonist of the cars movie, he is known to be kind, positive, courageous, and brave, and enjoys having fun and spending time with his friends. Prior to his arrival in Radiator Springs, Lightning was cocky and self-centered, only caring about two things: winning and the pride that comes with it.

  • Mater (Larry the Cable Guy)

    • Mater is a deuteragonist and Lightning McQueen's bestfriend. Mater is extremely friendly, loving, selfless, loyal, and dimwitted.

  • Sally Carrera (Bonnie Hunt)

    • Sally Carrera is also a deuteragonist and is known to be Lightning McQueen's girlfriend. She is known to be kind and caring, and cares deeply for her friends. She takes the most interest in the history of the town, Radiator Springs.

  • Doc Hudson (Paul Newman)

    • Doc Hudson is another deuteragonist and is portrayed as a grumpy, but fatherly old man who wants to leave his racing career behind after the painful rejection of the racing world and stay out of the public line.
